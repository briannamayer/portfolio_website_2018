About Me

My artwork primarily focuses on depicting the inner mind through self portraits. The strongest elements in my art are the value/tone, space, color and shape; while the mediums used are colored pencils, oil paint, charcoal and pastel. Throughout my art I wanted to convey what I can’t with words. My subject matter relatively stays the same by revolving around myself and the conflicts within as well as learning to deal with the past.
